Kroger's Taste of Italy Microsite aimed to inspire through authentic regional recipes and ingredients. A large portion of the campaign and UX was centered around the idea of "building an authentic 10 course meal".

Context

Kroger was looking to expand and improve on their first iteration of "Taste of Italy". A key strategy was to brand themselves as an expert in food. A large feature of the site was the "Meal Builder" in which users could learn and create an authentic 10 course meal. This feature underwent several iterations of prototyping and user testing.
